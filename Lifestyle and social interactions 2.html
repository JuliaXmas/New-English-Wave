Is it necessary to develop communication skills?

Searching for solutions?

Let's say two colleagues on the same team are constantly in conflict due to misunderstandings of each other. One of them often expresses his thoughts and ideas very directly and categorically, which often offends the second colleague, who perceives this as criticism and aggression. Instead of discussing the problem and trying to find mutual understanding, both colleagues withdraw into themselves and continue to argue, which creates a tense atmosphere in the team and makes it difficult to complete work tasks. As a result, the team suffers from unproductive communication and lack of constructive interaction between colleagues.
